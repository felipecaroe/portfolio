Project Overview

Impulse™ is a speculative Case Study on designing the future of contextual finance, through the macro-trends Artificial Intelligence, Personal Financial Agent and Smart devices.

The Problem Statement

Fintech aims for 'frictionless payments', often leading to impulsive spending. I found a deeper issue: the gap between inspiration and responsible financial decisions. The challenge is not just removing friction, but adding thoughtful guidance, empowering users with intelligent choices without disrupting the moment.

The technology

This concept was developed to be a core experience within the Even G1 AR glasses ecosystem. It leverages the G1's native hardware capabilities in tandem with a sophisticated, hybrid AI architecture to deliver a proactive, personal financial co-pilot directly within the user's field of view.

In 2025 we saw many advances in technology and concept: AR Glasses became Smart Glasses, focused on enhance the experience without interruption. Meta, Apple and other players are investing on it. At the same time, OpenAI launched Artificial Intelligence Financial Agents, assets that are almost completely autonomous in purchasing activities.

What I aimed to create here was a seamless journey combining those technologies with the best experience a user can have, delivering contextual financial services and financial education for a more responsible behavior.

Even G1 AR Glasses use HAOS™: Holistic Adaptive Optical System. Tiny optical projectors beam digital content in front of you, without sacrificing the visual clarity of your glasses.

Even G1 is everyday eyewear. Not gadgetry. They simply blend an extra, customisable layer of digital functionality that elevates your view of the world. Without interrupting reality.

Micro-LED optical engines transmit content onto a pair of waveguide lenses, which then project it about two meters in front of you. Features like translation, navigation, teleprompt, A.I. and more.

Step by step
These are the steps on the Financial Education assistant's journey:
The experience begins with a calm interface, designed to augment reality, not obstruct it. The display is minimal, showing only essential data like time and weather, plus contextual information like the current song playing on Spotify.
It starts with a trigger. When your gaze lingers on an object, the on-device AI registers your focus. This visual cue, combined with biometric data from a connected watch detecting a spike in interest, activates Impulse.
Once activated, Impulse performs two actions. First, it uses cloud-based visual recognition to identify the object. Second, it scans a network of retailers for availability and pricing. A notification then appears in your view, prompting you to look up for details.
This is the pathway, turned into action. Impulse presents a personalized savings plan, calculated from your budget via Open Banking. A simple voice command—"Start the plan"—is all it takes to initiate. For maximum security, the final authorization is a quick biometric approval on your paired smartphone to respect PSD2/SCA requirements.
After some time, the user is in a different setting, like working. A subtle notification appears in their view. The notification card is titled Impulse: Savings Update and shows a progress chart that is about 45% full. Below the chart, an encouraging message reads: You've saved 2,250 € for your "Objective Harley"! Keep on it!

Settings
Impulse™ is your financial co-pilot, putting you in control. Use these settings to adjust how your assistant interacts with you, ensuring a helpful and tailored experience. It can be tuned automatically through AI or manually through the companion app.
Impulse™ Financial Assistant
Manage your Impulse™ experience here. Configure the assistant's mode, adjust AI behaviors, teach it your interests, and access quick controls.
Impulse™ Configuration
Choose the best mode for your context: Manual Mode: Impulse™ activates with a gesture. Intelligent Mode: Impulse™ learns when to engage. Discovery Mode: Ideal for shopping.
Proactivity Settings
Go beyond the main modes and customize details on assistant behavior and communication. Suggestion Frequency: This slider limits proactive suggestions. Voice Notifications: Announce suggestions via audio. Adaptive Learning: Impulse™ learns from your feedback to improve suggestions.
Interest Profile
Teach Impulse™ what you love. Setting priorities helps AI focus on what matters, making suggestions relevant. Priority Levels: High: Enthusiast, proactive suggestions. Medium: Interested, balanced suggestions. Low: Passing interest, exceptional opportunities only.
Quick Actions

This is your screen for direct, in-the-moment control over your assistant, as well as advanced customization for power users. Pause Impulse™: The "do not disturb" button disables proactive suggestions with a tap, ideal for meetings. View History: Access a log of all suggestions and actions by Impulse™. Manage and delete past interactions. Advanced Settings: Remap voice commands and gestures for a custom experience.

Architecture
The entire Impulse™ Ecosystem connects various players and APIs, working together effortlessly to deliver a truly seamless experience. Bank account details align with user settings and preferences, utilizing edge computing on wearable devices to identify opportunities across different merchants.
